Chief of Agents

One interface for a fleet of AI agents.

You run a large fleet of specialist agents. Instead of monitoring them, a Chief of Agents aggregates everything they do and surfaces only the digests — and the decisions that need a human. Decision-first, human-in-the-loop by default.
